Name of Quantlet: CO_diagnostic_regression

Published in: Recalibrating Tail Event Forecasts under Temporal Dependence

Description: OLS diagnostic regression of Quantile Score improvement (ΔQS) on conformal threshold (qV_stat) and raw violation rate (π̂_raw) across 240 model–asset pairs at α=0.01. Reports coefficients with homoskedastic, asset-clustered, and model-clustered standard errors. Computes partial R² for qV_stat via Frisch–Waugh–Lovell.

Keywords: conformal prediction, VaR, quantile score, diagnostic regression, clustered standard errors, partial R-squared

Author: Daniel Traian Pele

Datafile: cfp_ijf_data/paper_outputs/tables/all_results.csv, cfp_ijf_data/paper_outputs/tables/moirai11_results.csv
