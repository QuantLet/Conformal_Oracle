Name of Quantlet: CFP_ES_Correction_Z2

Published in: 'Distribution-Free Recalibration of Tail Quantile Forecasts under Temporal Dependence'

Description: Computes the heuristic Expected Shortfall correction from Appendix C
             and evaluates the Acerbi-Szekely Z2 backtest statistic before and after
             correction across 9 forecasters and 24 financial assets.
             Implements Definition C.2 (conformal ES correction) and reports Table C.1.

Keywords: conformal prediction, Expected Shortfall, Z2 backtest, Acerbi-Szekely, VaR, ES correction, tail risk

See also:
- CO_full_evaluation
- CFP_Capital_Charge

Author: Daniel Traian Pele, Stefan Lessmann, Wolfgang Karl Haerdle

Submitted: 2026-04-09

Input: cfp_ijf_data/returns/*.csv, cfp_ijf_data/{chronos_small,chronos_mini,timesfm25,moirai2,lagllama}/*.parquet, cfp_ijf_data/benchmarks/*.parquet

Output: table_c1_es_correction.csv, LaTeX table block (Table C.1)

Example: Z2 drops from +131 (raw) to +11 (corrected) for Chronos-Small, confirming the heuristic ES correction absorbs the bulk of excess tail violations.
