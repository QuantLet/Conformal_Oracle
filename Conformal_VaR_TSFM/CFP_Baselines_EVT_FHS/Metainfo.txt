Name of Quantlet: CFP_Baselines_EVT_FHS

Published in: 'Distribution-Free Recalibration of Tail Quantile Forecasts under Temporal Dependence'

Description: Computes EVT Peaks-over-Threshold (McNeil and Frey 2000) and Filtered
             Historical Simulation (Barone-Adesi et al. 1999) VaR baselines across
             24 financial assets with daily GARCH re-estimation on 250-day rolling
             windows. Reports violation rates, Kupiec tests, Quantile Scores, and
             Basel Green Zone rates for Table 12 of the paper.

Keywords: EVT, Peaks-over-Threshold, GPD, Filtered Historical Simulation, GARCH, VaR, backtesting, Basel

See also:
- CO_full_evaluation
- CO_baseline_comparison

Author: Daniel Traian Pele, Stefan Lessmann, Wolfgang Karl Haerdle

Submitted: 2026-04-11

Input: cfp_ijf_data/returns/*.csv (24 assets, daily log returns)

Output: baselines_evt_fhs.csv, baselines_evt_fhs_summary.csv
