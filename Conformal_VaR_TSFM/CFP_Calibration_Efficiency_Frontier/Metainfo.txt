Name of Quantlet: CFP_Calibration_Efficiency_Frontier

Published in: 'Distribution-Free Recalibration of Tail Quantile Forecasts under Temporal Dependence'

Description: Generates the two-panel calibration-efficiency frontier (Figure 3)
             showing the trade-off between empirical coverage and VaR width for
             four representative forecasters (Chronos-Small, TimesFM 2.5, Lag-Llama,
             GJR-GARCH) before and after conformal correction. Panel (a) shows the
             zoomed view near the 99% target; panel (b) shows the full scale
             including Chronos-Small at raw coverage ~61%.

Keywords: calibration efficiency, VaR width, coverage, conformal prediction, frontier, time series foundation models

See also:
- CO_full_evaluation

Author: Daniel Traian Pele, Stefan Lessmann, Wolfgang Karl Haerdle

Submitted: 2026-04-09

Input: cfp_ijf_data/paper_outputs/tables/all_results.csv

Output: calibration_efficiency_frontier_v2.pdf, calibration_efficiency_frontier_v2.png

Example: Chronos-Small moves from raw coverage 61.2% (width 0.003) to corrected coverage 98.9% (width 0.041), while TimesFM requires only a modest shift from 98.6% to 99.0%.
