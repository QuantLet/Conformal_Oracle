Name of Quantlet: CO_full_evaluation

Published in: 'Distribution-Free Recalibration of Tail Quantile Forecasts under Temporal Dependence'

Description: Main evaluation pipeline for conformal VaR recalibration.
             Downloads 24 asset return series, generates forecasts from 5 TSFMs
             (Chronos-Small, Chronos-Mini, TimesFM 2.5, Moirai 1.1-R, Lag-Llama)
             and 4 parametric benchmarks (GJR-GARCH, GARCH-N, HS, EWMA),
             runs one-sided conformal calibration (70/30 split),
             produces Tables 1-8 and Figures 1-5 of the paper.

Keywords: conformal prediction, Value-at-Risk, foundation models, backtesting, Basel

See also:
- CO_simulation_study
- CO_rolling_conformal
- CO_baseline_comparison

Author: Daniel Traian Pele, Stefan Lessmann, Wolfgang Karl Haerdle

Submitted: 2026-03-26

Input: Yahoo Finance daily prices (24 assets, 2000-2026)

Output: 8 LaTeX tables, 9 PDF figures, all parquet forecasts and backtest results

Example:
- CFP_Data_Download.ipynb: download 24 asset returns
- CFP_Chronos_Forecasts.ipynb: Chronos-Small/Mini VaR forecasts
- CFP_TimesFM_Forecasts.ipynb: TimesFM 2.5 quantile-to-VaR
- CFP_Moirai_Forecasts.ipynb: Moirai 1.1-R VaR forecasts
- CFP_LagLlama_Forecasts.ipynb: Lag-Llama VaR forecasts
- CFP_Parametric_Benchmarks.ipynb: GJR-GARCH, GARCH-N, HS, EWMA
- CFP_Conformal_Calibration.ipynb: core calibration and backtesting
- CFP_qV_Diagnostic.ipynb: conformal threshold analysis
- CFP_Multi_Quantile.ipynb: multi-alpha evaluation
- CFP_Panel_Pooled.ipynb: cross-asset pooled backtest
- CFP_Scoring_Rules.ipynb: Quantile Score and DM tests
- CFP_Traffic_Light.ipynb: Basel traffic light heatmaps
- CFP_Paper_Tables_Figures.ipynb: publication-ready outputs
