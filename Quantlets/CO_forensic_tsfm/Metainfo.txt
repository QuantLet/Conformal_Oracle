Name of Quantlet: CO_forensic_tsfm

Published in: Recalibrating Tail Risk Forecasts under Temporal Dependence

Description: Forensic validation of quantile-grid TSFM tail failures. Four-panel figure showing realised S&P 500 returns versus predicted quantiles for TimesFM 2.5 and Moirai 2.0 over 2022. Top row: native 10th percentile and extrapolated 1st percentile against returns. Bottom row: full predicted quantile structure (1%, 2.5%, 5%, 10% quantiles and mean). Central quantiles track returns; the extrapolated 1% tail does not. Empirical violation rates at the mean (49.7% TimesFM, 50.2% Moirai) confirm correct interpretation; the failure is specific to the 1% tail.

Keywords: conformal prediction, VaR, time series foundation models, forensic validation, quantile grid, tail failure, TSFM
